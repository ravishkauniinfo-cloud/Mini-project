About Us

Discover the mission behind MathHub.

The Ultimate Specification Library

MathHub is a beautifully designed, all-in-one digital mathematics platform. Our mission is to provide students, professionals, and enthusiasts with a unified, native suite of mathematical tools that are both incredibly powerful and visually stunning.

We've eliminated the need to constantly bounce between different websites and clunky, outdated apps. Whether you are solving standard everyday arithmetic, graphing complex algebraic equations, performing advanced matrix algebra, converting number systems on the fly, or evaluating calculus integrals—MathHub handles it all with zero server lag right inside your browser.

Experience a seamless, interactive learning environment wrapped in a premium, ultra-responsive "Liquid Glass" design framework. Mathematics has never looked this good.

Help Center

Learn how to use each tool within the MathHub platform.

Graphing Calculator

Click the "+ Add" button to create a new equation field. Type functions using standard notation like sin(x) or x^2 + 5. The interactive Plotly canvas will automatically draw your curves. You can click and drag the canvas to pan, and use your mouse wheel or the on-screen buttons to seamlessly zoom in and out.

Scientific Calculator

Use the intuitive multi-tab keypad (Main, Func, ABC) to input complex mathematical formulas. You can evaluate advanced trigonometric, statistical, and algebraic expressions natively. Switch between Radians (RAD) and Degrees (DEG) using the toggle at the top right of the keypad.

Matrix Calculator

Click "New Matrix" to spawn a matrix editor. Adjust the rows and columns using the control panel, then type your numbers into the grid. Once defined, you can use the left-hand evaluation panel to perform operations like Matrix Multiplication (A*B), Transpositions (Aᵀ), or calculating Determinants (det(A)).

Unit Converter

Select a measurement category from the top tabs (Length, Temperature, Area, Volume, etc.). Choose your base unit in the left column and your target unit in the right column. Type a number into either input box, and the conversion will calculate instantly in both directions.

Number System

Select your preferred bit-length from the top dropdown (8-bit up to 64-bit). You can toggle individual bits visually by clicking the 0s and 1s in the interactive table, or you can type directly into the Decimal, Binary, Hexadecimal, or Octal input fields to see everything update synchronously.

∫ Calculus

Toggle between Differentiation and Integration. For Differentiation: Enter a function and click "Generate Graph". The engine will output simplified latex for f'(x) and f''(x), plot all three curves, and automatically mark detected Inflection Points, Local Max/Mins, and Roots on the canvas. For Integration: Evaluate indefinite or definite integrals with precise bound inputs.

Misc Calculators

Access a suite of everyday utility calculators. Evaluate your BMI, forecast Compound Interest over time, estimate your monthly Mortgage payments, or calculate a cumulative GPA. Every miscellaneous calculator dynamically displays the exact mathematical equation used to reach the final answer.
